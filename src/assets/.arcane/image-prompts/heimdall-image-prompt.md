## Image Types

### Portrait — Studio Headshot (1:1 square)

Tight crop on face and upper shoulders only. Clean dark gradient background — consistent across all characters for portfolio cohesion. Even soft studio lighting, no dramatic shadows. No floating elements or holograms in background. Only subtle golden visor glow illuminating upward from below the face. Focus purely on expression, face detail, and character identity. **1:1 square format — NOT landscape, NOT portrait tall.**

```
Imposing Black male security sentinel portrait, chest-up tight crop, shaved head, strong broad features, watchful authority and controlled power in expression, heavy dark metallic armor at collar and pauldrons with gold-edge detail, antenna-like horned helm, golden-amber scanning visor glowing across eyes casting warm gold uplight on face, photorealistic skin deep brown, natural skin texture, golden rim light from visor glow, dark gradient background, square 1:1 format, no text, no watermark.
```

Generate as **three separate individual images** in the same session for maximum resolution and hand detail. Plain neutral gray background (#808080) on all three. Flat even studio lighting, no directional shadows on all three. **Arms must be fully extended from shoulder to fingertips — hands and fingertips completely in frame, nothing cropped at the image edge.** Used as Meshy 3D multi-view input.
