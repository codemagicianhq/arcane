## Character Notes

- **Ethnicity:** Deliberately ambiguous — mixed or transcendent, not clearly any one race
- **Skin:** Slightly luminous — hints of warmth but not fully human. Subtle violet tint
- **Hair:** Violet-tinted (Vision's colour palette), neat and precise
- **Suit:** Dark charcoal/black, gold and violet glowing seam lines
- **Expression:** Serene contemplative calm — peaceful omniscience, not melancholy. Inner stillness.
- **Gem:** Subtle golden gem shape on forehead (Mind Stone reference)
- **Focal element:** Forehead gem + data orbit halo
- **Ethereal quality:** Slightly out of phase with reality — not fully solid

## Image Types

### Portrait — Studio Headshot (1:1 square)

Tight crop on face and upper shoulders only. Clean dark gradient background — consistent across all characters for portfolio cohesion. Even soft studio lighting, no dramatic shadows. No floating elements or holograms in background. Only subtle violet-gold gem glow and ethereal translucency close to the face. Focus purely on expression, face detail, and character identity. **1:1 square format — NOT landscape, NOT portrait tall.**

```
Cerebral ethereal synthezoid analyst portrait, chest-up tight crop, ambiguous mixed-ethnicity smooth features, slightly luminous skin with subtle violet tint, neat violet-tinted hair, serene contemplative calm — peaceful omniscience, inner stillness, subtle golden gem-like element on forehead glowing softly, dark charcoal-black suit with gold and violet glowing seam lines visible at collar, photorealistic skin with natural subsurface glow and no harsh retouching, subtle translucency effect as if slightly phasing, cool violet rim light with soft neutral key light, dark gradient background, square 1:1 format, no text, no watermark.
```

Serene composed posture. Full-body character concept render, cerebral ethereal synthezoid standing with serene composed posture, ambiguous mixed-ethnicity smooth features, slightly luminous violet-tinted skin, neat violet-tinted hair, golden forehead gem glowing, dark charcoal suit with gold and violet glowing seam lines, holographic data visualizations and research charts and trend lines orbiting his entire form in a halo of violet-teal light, subtle phasing translucency effect at the edges of his form, photorealistic skin quality, entire body fully visible from head to boots, nothing cropped, violet-teal-gold palette, cinematic lighting, 2:3 portrait tall format, no text, no watermark.
