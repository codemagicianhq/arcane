## Image Types

### Portrait — Studio Headshot (1:1 square)

Tight crop on face and upper shoulders only. Clean dark gradient background — consistent across all characters for portfolio cohesion. Even soft studio lighting, no dramatic shadows. No floating elements or holograms in background. Only subtle amber-blue chronometric glow from the forearm device close to the face. Focus purely on expression, face detail, and character identity. **1:1 square format — NOT landscape, NOT portrait tall.**

```
Mediterranean/Southern European woman collaboration lead portrait, chest-up tight crop, olive skin, dark wavy hair, athletic-lean build, calm diplomatic confidence with knowing warmth — she has already mapped the path forward, tactical dark suit at collar and shoulders with amber and blue-teal chronometric accent lines, compact circular forearm device glowing warmly at the edge of frame, photorealistic skin with natural texture and no skin smoothing, warm amber rim light with cool cyan accent, dark gradient background, square 1:1 format, no text, no watermark.
```
