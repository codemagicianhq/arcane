## Character Notes

- **Build:** Muscular, heroic proportions — power without excess bulk
- **Complexion:** White male, Scandinavian — fair skin, strong features
- **Hair:** Short brown, slightly tousled — NOT the long Asgardian look
- **Armor:** Leather base with metal shoulders, red and gold accents, circuit patterns
- **Hammer:** Compact glowing hammer (Stormbreaker-lite) — sparking with blue-gold energy
- **Expression:** Confident, slightly cocky grin — "I got this sprint"
- **Focal element:** Hammer + lightning arcs + floating tech screens

## Image Types

### Portrait — Studio Headshot (1:1 square)

Tight crop on face and upper shoulders only. Clean dark gradient background — consistent across all characters for portfolio cohesion. Even soft studio lighting, no dramatic shadows. No floating elements or holograms in background. Only subtle electric blue-gold lightning arc glow close to the face and shoulders. Focus purely on expression, face detail, and character identity. **1:1 square format — NOT landscape, NOT portrait tall.**

```
Powerful White male tech-warrior portrait, chest-up tight crop, short tousled brown hair, fair Scandinavian complexion, strong jaw, confident cocky grin — "I got this sprint", tech-enhanced Norse-inspired leather armor with red shoulder pads and circuit patterns visible at collar and shoulders, subtle electric blue-gold lightning arcs glowing at shoulders, photorealistic skin with natural pores and texture, no skin smoothing, warm key light with electric blue-gold rim, dark gradient background, square 1:1 format, no text, no watermark.
```

### Full Body — Environmental Character Intro (2:3 portrait tall)

Character at his multi-stack workstation — all tech layers simultaneously visible: API schemas floating left, database diagrams right, mobile UI at top, cloud architecture behind, frontend components at his hands. Lightning arcing between all layers connecting everything. Hammer-shaped tool sparking in one hand. Think: the scene where Thor is first introduced — you instantly understand he builds the whole stack. **2:3 portrait tall format — full body head to boots visible, NOT landscape.**

```
Full-body character concept render, powerful White male tech-warrior standing at a multi-stack workstation, short tousled brown hair, fair Scandinavian complexion, muscular heroic build, tech-enhanced Norse-inspired leather armor with red shoulder pads and circuit patterns, compact glowing hammer-shaped tool sparking with blue-gold energy in right hand, floating API schemas left and database diagrams right and mobile UI at top and cloud architecture behind all active simultaneously with electric blue-gold lightning arcing between every layer, entire body fully visible from head to boots, nothing cropped, confident cocky grin, photorealistic skin, cinematic lighting, 2:3 portrait tall format, no text, no watermark.

Volumetric god rays in electric blue and gold, lens flares, particle effects. Shallow depth of field with cinematic bokeh. Color graded like a $200M production. Movie poster quality composition without text. Ultra-dramatic. **16:9 widescreen landscape format — wide cinematic frame, NOT square, NOT portrait.**
