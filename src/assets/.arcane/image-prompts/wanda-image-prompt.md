## Character Notes

- **Ethnicity:** Anglo-Australian — fair skin, golden blonde hair, blue eyes
- **Hair:** Long golden blonde — distinct from other female agents (Brazilian, Mediterranean, Latina)
- **Attire:** Professional dark business attire — NOT the Scarlet Witch tiara/crown
- **Energy:** Scarlet-red chaos magic — flowing, controlled, reshaping content
- **Expression:** Composed authority with a knowing half-smile — persuasion personified, she's already won
- **Props:** Floating text, ad copy, campaign visuals being reshaped by energy
- **Focal element:** Scarlet energy hands + floating content
- **Important:** No tiara, no crown — this is a professional avatar, not cosplay

## Image Types

### Portrait — Studio Headshot (1:1 square)

Tight crop on face and upper shoulders only. Clean dark gradient background — consistent across all characters for portfolio cohesion. Even soft studio lighting, no dramatic shadows. No floating elements or holograms in background. Only subtle scarlet energy glow close to the hands and face. Focus purely on expression, face detail, and character identity. **1:1 square format — NOT landscape, NOT portrait tall.**

```
Anglo-Australian woman marketing strategist portrait, chest-up tight crop, long golden blonde hair, bright blue eyes, knowing half-smile — she's already seen 10 moves ahead, professional dark tailored attire at collar and shoulders — absolutely no tiara or crown, subtle scarlet energy tendrils glowing warmly at hands just in frame, photorealistic skin with natural texture and no skin smoothing, warm golden rim light from the hair with cool scarlet energy glow, dark gradient background, square 1:1 format, no text, no watermark.
```

### Full Body — Environmental Character Intro (2:3 portrait tall)

Character standing in her strategy workspace, scarlet energy tendrils actively reshaping floating ad copy, campaign visuals, and text around her. Composed authoritative posture, knowing half-smile. Think: the scene where Wanda is first introduced — you instantly understand she controls the narrative. **2:3 portrait tall format — full body head to boots visible, NOT landscape.**

```
Full-body character concept render, Anglo-Australian woman marketing strategist standing in a creative strategy workspace, long golden blonde hair, bright blue eyes, knowing half-smile, professional dark tailored attire — jacket and trousers, absolutely no tiara or crown of any kind, both hands raised with scarlet energy tendrils pouring from them and actively reshaping floating ad copy, campaign visuals, brand imagery, and content fragments swirling around her in a vortex, photorealistic skin, entire body fully visible from head to boots, nothing cropped, warm scarlet-gold palette against dark background, cinematic lighting, 2:3 portrait tall format, no text, no watermark.
```

Ultra-dramatic. **16:9 widescreen landscape format — wide cinematic frame, NOT square, NOT portrait.**
