Tight crop on face and upper shoulders only. Clean dark gradient background — consistent across all characters for portfolio cohesion. Even soft studio lighting, no dramatic shadows. No floating elements or holograms in background. Only subtle warm amber engine-room glow and green status light close to the face. Focus purely on expression, face detail, and character identity. **1:1 square format — NOT landscape, NOT portrait tall.**
